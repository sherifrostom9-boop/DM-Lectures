Interactive Mock-ups

You can create free interactive web mockups using various online tools that offer website mockup templates and easy drag-and-drop functionality.

Why Use Mock-ups?

  • Visualize your designs in realistic contexts
  • Present professional concepts to clients
  • Test user interface ideas before development
  • Create portfolio pieces and case studies

Popular Tools

  • Canva - Easy templates and drag-and-drop
  • Mockplus - Interactive UI components
  • Moqups - Wireframes with icon library
  • Mockey - AI-powered generator
  • MockupBro - High-quality, no watermark
Workshop Focus: In this lecture, we'll explore three powerful free mockup tools: Canva, Mockey, and MockupBro. Each offers unique features for creating professional website and app mockups.

Workshop 1: Canva

Canva

Design Platform with Mock-up Templates

Canva is a versatile design platform that offers a dedicated mockups section with professional templates for computers, smartphones, tablets, and other devices. Perfect for beginners and professionals alike.

How to Create Mock-ups in Canva

Step 1

Access the Mockups Section

Navigate to the "Mockups" section in Canva's interface. Here you'll find a wide variety of device templates including:

  • Desktop computers and laptops
  • Smartphones (iPhone, Android)
  • Tablets and iPads
  • Smartwatches and wearables
  • Multiple device combinations
Step 2

Choose Your Device Template

Select the device that best represents where your design will be displayed. Consider your target audience and the primary platform for your website or application.

Step 3

Upload Your Design

Upload a screenshot or design file of your website or app interface. Canva accepts various formats including PNG, JPG, and PDF files.

Step 4

Drag and Adjust

Drag your uploaded design into the mockup template. Canva automatically fits it to the screen, but you can make manual adjustments to:

  • Scale and resize the design
  • Position it correctly on the screen
  • Adjust perspective and angles
  • Fine-tune alignment
Step 5

Customize and Download

Make final adjustments to backgrounds, add text overlays or branding elements, and download your professional mockup in high resolution.

Pro Tip: Canva offers both free and premium templates. Free templates provide excellent quality for most projects, while premium templates offer more sophisticated designs and device options.

Workshop 2: Mockey

Mockey

AI-Powered Mockup Generator

Mockey is a free AI mockup generator that uses artificial intelligence to create realistic and professional mockups. It offers smart automation features that simplify the mockup creation process.

How to Use Mockey

Step 1

Sign Up for Free Account

Create a free account on Mockey's platform. The registration process is quick and gives you access to:

  • Thousands of mockup templates
  • AI-powered smart fitting
  • Cloud storage for your projects
  • Regular template updates
Step 2

Choose a Template

Browse through Mockey's extensive template library. Categories include:

  • Technology devices (computers, phones, tablets)
  • Print materials (business cards, brochures)
  • Apparel and merchandise
  • Packaging and product mockups
  • Environmental and contextual scenes
Step 3

Upload Your Design

Upload your website screenshot or app interface. Mockey's AI automatically analyzes your design and suggests the best fitting options.

Step 4

Customize with AI Assistance

Use Mockey's AI-powered tools to customize your mockup:

  • Auto-fit design to device screens
  • Smart perspective adjustment
  • Background replacement options
  • Lighting and shadow effects
  • Color scheme adjustments
Step 5

Download Your Mockup

Once satisfied with your customizations, download the final mockup in high resolution. Mockey offers various export options and formats.

AI Advantage: Mockey's artificial intelligence automatically detects screen boundaries and applies realistic perspectives, saving you time on manual adjustments while ensuring professional results.
