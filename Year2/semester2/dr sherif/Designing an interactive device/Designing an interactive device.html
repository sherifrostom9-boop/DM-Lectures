Designing an Interactive Device

From Concept to Working Prototype • 30 Hours

Course Overview

This hands-on course guides you through the complete process of designing and building interactive physical devices. You will learn user-centered design, rapid prototyping, hardware-software integration, ethical considerations, and final delivery of a working interactive product.

Perfect for students who want to turn ideas into tangible, user-friendly interactive systems.
