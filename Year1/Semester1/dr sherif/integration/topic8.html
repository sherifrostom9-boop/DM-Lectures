Audio & Multimedia

Integrating sound and video content with cross-browser compatibility.

The <audio> Element

Used to play sound files like MP3 or OGG. The controls attribute adds play, pause, and volume buttons.

<audio controls>
  <source src="horse.mp3" type="audio/mpeg">
  Your browser does not support audio.
</audio>
  • controls: Displays the audio player interface.
  • <source>: Allows you to provide multiple file formats.

The <video> Element

Similar to audio, but used for visual media. You should always include width and height attributes.

<video width="320" height="240" controls>
  <source src="movie.mp4" type="video/mp4">
  Your browser does not support video.
</video>

Multimedia Best Practices

To ensure your media plays on all devices, follow these rules:

  • Fallbacks: Always include text between the tags (e.g., "Your browser does not support...") for older browsers.
  • Multiple Sources: The browser will use the first recognized format it finds.
  • Autoplay Note: Most modern browsers block autoplay unless the media is also muted.
