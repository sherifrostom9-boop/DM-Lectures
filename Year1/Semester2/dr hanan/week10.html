Brand Strategy Final Pitch

Workshop Purpose

Demonstrate the integration of strategy, creativity, digital execution, and measurement into a cohesive brand system developed throughout the course.

Pitch Requirements (4 Dimensions)

1. Strategy

  • Positioning Statement
  • Target Audience
  • Consumer Insight

2. Identity

  • Logo Draft/Sketch
  • Color Rationale
  • Digital Adaptability

3. Digital

  • Hero Content Piece
  • Channel Role Strategy
  • Execution Mockup

4. Metrics

  • Success KPIs
  • Tracking Methods
  • Logic of Evaluation

Peer Review Framework

Analyze presentations using the "I Like, I Wish, I Wonder" model to provide constructive strategic critique.

I Like (Strong Point)
I Wish (Improvement)
I Wonder (Question)

Course Wrap-Up Insight

Strong brands are systems, not just campaigns. Consistency builds trust, creativity must be rooted in strategy, and measurement completes the loop. You are now ready to think like a strategist, act like a digital native, and lead as a brand visionary.
